Welcome to the Toast Master Script Learning Tool

This tool is designed to help you learn your scripts, in a fun and interactive way.

  1. Copy-paste your script (or drag and drop it)
  2. Click "Learn"
  3. Read the paragraph and click next
  4. When you finish reading all the paragraph, it will start again, but a few words will be missing, read it filling the gaps by memory.
  5. The amount of gaps will grow with every round
  6. If you need a tip, just click on any placeholder and the word will show up
